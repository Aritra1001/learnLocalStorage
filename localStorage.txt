Local Storage is a browser storage where we store data in the form of key-value pairs using an object.

localStorage.setItem(key, value) - To store and update data.

localStorage.getItem(key) - Gives us the value of that key.
 
localStorage.key(index) - Gives us the key on that index.

localStorage.length - Gives us the length of the data.

localStorage.removeItem(key) - Removes the key and its value.

localStorage.clear() - Deletes all the data.
